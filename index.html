System Mechanics

Rule-based rounds. Transparent outcomes.

How It Works

Round Structure

  • Each round lasts exactly 1 minute
  • Every round contains 9 boxes
  • Deploy into one or multiple boxes
  • Maximum 1 SOL per user per box

Round Outcome

  • One box selected via on-chain randomness
  • All SOL from all boxes forms a single pool
  • Participants in winning box claim proportional share
  • If no winner, pool rolls over to next round
  • Claims available anytime after round ends

Outcomes are random and cannot be predicted or influenced.

Monthly Distribution Cycle

XP accumulates throughout the month. At month's end, the protocol may distribute based on XP. XP then resets for the new cycle.
